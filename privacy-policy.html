1. Information Collected

Sudoku Pro is designed to be simple and privacy-friendly. We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as name, email, phone number). However, certain third-party services may collect limited technical data as described below:

  • Advertising Data (Google AdMob): The App displays banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may automatically collect:
    • IP address
    • Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
    • App usage analytics
    • Crash or performance logs
    Learn more at: Google Advertising Policies.
  • In-App Purchase Data (Google Play Billing): When you purchase the “Remove Ads” premium feature, the transaction is processed securely by Google Play Billing. Sudoku Pro itself never receives your credit card details or personal payment information. Learn more at: Google Play Billing Policy.
  • Firebase Services: Sudoku Pro uses Firebase Remote Config and Firebase Analytics to improve app performance and deliver feature updates. Firebase may collect anonymized statistical data such as:
    • Device type & OS version
    • Generic app usage patterns
    • Non-identifying analytics events
    • Error reports & diagnostic logs
    No personally identifiable information is stored by Sudoku Pro. Learn more at: Firebase Privacy & Security.
  • Performance & Crash Data: To improve stability, third-party services may automatically collect anonymous crash data and error logs. This data does not include personal information.

We do not sell, trade, or share any personal data with third parties.

2. Children’s Privacy

Sudoku Pro is intended for general audiences. It is not specifically directed at children under 13. Children under 13 should use the App only with parental or guardian supervision.

3. Offline Use

The game can be played offline. No gameplay data is transmitted to external servers by the Developer. However, ads and purchases require an Internet connection.

5. Your Choices & Data Rights

  • You may disable personalized ads in your Google account settings.
  • You may toggle personalized ads from the App’s settings.
  • Uninstalling the App deletes all locally stored preferences.
  • As no personal data is collected by the Developer, no data retention or deletion requests are required.
